Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

CashKey Property Group ("CashKey," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information you provide when visiting our website or communicating with us.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property address or details
  • Information submitted through forms or communications

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and requests
  • Provide cash offers or real estate-related services
  • Communicate with you about your request
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share personal information for targeted advertising.

Text Messaging (SMS) Communications

If you provide your phone number, you may receive SMS or voice communications related to your inquiry, including follow-ups, appointment confirmations, or service updates.

  • Message frequency may vary
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP
  • For help, reply HELP

Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase.

Data Protection & Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. While no method of transmission is 100% secure, we strive to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party tools such as analytics, form processors, or communication platforms to operate efficiently. These providers are only permitted to use information as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site usage, improve functionality, and analyze performance. You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your information, or opt out of certain communications. We do not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
